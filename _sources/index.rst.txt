=================
Welcome to UNITED
=================

An UNIfied TEmporal Data representation, reasoning, and predictions framework.


Features
========

* **Unified API** for Temporal AI solutions: easy-to-use, easy-to-extend.
* **Support for a variety of learning tasks** including :doc:`time series forecasting <examples/electricity/forecasting>`, :doc:`time series classification/regression <examples/basic_motions/classification>`, :doc:`time series annotation <examples/human_activity_recognition/annotation>` tasks.
* **Support multiple input/output type** including continuous and discrete values.
* **Multiple model architectures** including encoder-decoder, encoder-only, decoder-only models.
